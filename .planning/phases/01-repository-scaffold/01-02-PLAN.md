**FILE 3 — `src/BaseApi.Service/BaseApi.Service.csproj`** (webapi, ProjectReference to Core)

Per W-04: This csproj does NOT include a `<UserSecretsId>` element. The previous draft hand-typed a GUID-shaped identifier; that's a hygiene smell because PROJECT.md Out of Scope explicitly defers auth/secrets to v2, so the UserSecretsId would have no consumer in Phase 1 and would mislead a v2 reader into thinking secrets were already wired.
