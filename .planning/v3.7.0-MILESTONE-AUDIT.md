## Milestone Definition of Done

> Make the autonomous execution loop (cron fire → dispatch → process → result → fan-out) **self-heal through transient L2 (Redis) outages without operator intervention.** A new multi-replica `Keeper` console reacts to the `Fault<EntryStepDispatch>` / `Fault<ExecutionResult>` events the execution-path consumers publish on retry-budget exhaustion, probes L2 health on a bounded loop, pauses the affected workflow's cron, re-injects recovered work to its origin (riding the receiver's `flag[H]` idempotency), resumes when no recoveries remain pending, and parks the genuinely unrecoverable in `keeper-dlq` for operator triage.

**Verdict: ACHIEVED.** The full recover-both-paths and give-up flows are proven live (Phase-39 close gate, 3× consecutive GREEN at 500 RealStack facts each, triple-SHA net-zero held). All five cross-phase wires connect at source level.
